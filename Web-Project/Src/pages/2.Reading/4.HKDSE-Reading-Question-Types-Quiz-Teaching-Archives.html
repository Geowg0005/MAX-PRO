📝 Passage 1: E-sports in Hong Kong

The rise of e-sports has transformed competitive gaming into a legitimate career path for many youths in Hong Kong. While traditionalists often dismiss this as a mere waste of time, the industry actually generated millions in revenue last year. Professional gamers dedicate up to ten hours a day to hone their reflexes. They also engage in physical exercises to prevent injuries like carpal tunnel syndrome, proving that the profession is more physically demanding than it appears on the surface.
Skill & Reference Question & Answer
Referencing / "this" (Line 2) Q1. What does "this" refer to? (hint) competitive gaming / e-sports
💡 Turning an action into a noun phrase.
🔊
Vocabulary / "acceptable" Q2. Find a word meaning "acceptable and valid": (word) legitimate 🔊
T/F/NG / Fitness Q3. Players do not need physical fitness to succeed. (T / F / NG) False
💡 The text states they "engage in physical exercises to prevent injuries".
🔊
Inferencing / Perception Q4. What does the final phrase imply about public perception? (reveal) The public incorrectly believes e-sports requires no physical effort. 🔊

📝 Passage 2: The Cost of Fast Fashion

Fast fashion has democratized style by making trendy clothes affordable for the masses. However, this accessibility comes at a severe environmental cost. Garments are often discarded after only a few wears, leading to overflowing landfills. Furthermore, the relentless production cycle pollutes local water sources in developing nations. To mitigate this growing crisis, environmentalists advocate for a shift towards a 'circular economy', where clothes are actively recycled and repurposed rather than simply thrown away.
Skill & Reference Question & Answer
Summary Cloze / Environment Q5. (i) Fast fashion allows the public to buy __________ (word) clothes. trendy
(ii) People quickly throw away their __________ (word). garments 🔊
Referencing / "accessibility" Q6. What does "this accessibility" refer to? (reveal) making trendy clothes affordable for the masses 🔊
T/F/NG / Location Q7. Damage only happens in developed countries. (T / F / NG) False
💡 Text mentions "developing nations".
🔊
Vocabulary / "mitigate" Q8. What does "mitigate" mean? (A. worse / B. ignore / C. less severe / D. celebrate) C. to make something less severe 🔊
